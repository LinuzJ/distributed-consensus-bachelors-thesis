\thesisabstract{
    The field of population protocols has seen recent progress in solving the consensus problem, specifically the approximate and exact majority problems. The Undecided State Protocol and Nonuniform-Majority Protocol were introduced as solutions for systems with $k = 2$ opinions, with the latter being optimized for time and space complexity. Generalizations were later developed for $k > 2$ opinions. 
}
